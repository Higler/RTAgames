About the Studio

Where craft meets conviction

Founded with a belief that games are art with purpose, Round Table Arts creates experiences rooted in strong narratives, diverse perspectives, and mechanics that respect the player's time and intelligence.

We're a small, focused team — collaborators across disciplines united around one round table, building worlds players want to return to.

01

Narrative First

Every mechanic serves the story. We build worlds with characters, stakes, and emotional arcs that linger.

02

Bold Systems

From time-travel combat to co-op horde survival, our games push genre boundaries without sacrificing clarity.

03

Community Built

We develop in the open with our Discord community — feedback shapes features, balance, and direction.
